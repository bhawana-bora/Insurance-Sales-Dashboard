# **BUSINESS REQUIREMENT DOCUMENT (BRD)**

## Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

**Client:** Life Insurance
**Project Type:** Power BI Analytics Implementation
**Prepared By:** Business Analyst
**Date:** March 2026

---

# 1. Executive Summary

This document outlines the business requirements for the development of an enterprise-level Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard using Power BI. The objective of this project is to provide leadership with real-time visibility into sales performance, agent productivity, product mix, policy persistency, claims ratio, and customer satisfaction (NPS).

The dashboard will support data-driven decision-making at executive, regional, and branch levels.

---

# 2. Business Background

The organization operates through multiple distribution channels including Bancassurance, Direct Sales, and Online channels across various regions in India.

Current challenges:

* Limited centralized visibility of sales KPIs
* Manual reporting through Excel
* Delayed performance insights
* No integrated view of Sales, Claims, and Customer NPS
* Difficulty in tracking policy lapse and persistency trends

---

# 3. Business Objectives

1. Improve monitoring of Gross Written Premium (GWP) and APE growth.
2. Increase policy persistency ratio (13th & 25th month).
3. Enhance agent productivity and regional accountability.
4. Reduce policy lapse rate.
5. Monitor claims ratio and settlement trends.
6. Improve customer satisfaction and NPS.

---

# 4. Project Scope

## 4.1 In Scope

* Sales performance dashboard
* Agent performance monitoring
* Product performance analysis
* Claims ratio tracking
* NPS tracking and segmentation
* Time intelligence reporting (YTD, MoM, QoQ, YoY)
* Drill-down capability (Region → Branch → Agent → Policy)

## 4.2 Out of Scope

* Changes to core policy administration systems
* Real-time transaction processing
* Regulatory compliance reporting
* External partner reporting integrations

# 6. Requirement Analysis

## 6.1 Functional Requirements

FR-01: Dashboard must display executive summary with core KPIs.
FR-02: Users must be able to filter by Region, Branch, Product, Date, and Channel.
FR-03: Drill-down functionality from Region → Branch → Agent.
FR-04: Dashboard must calculate APE based on payment mode.
FR-05: Persistency Ratio must be calculated for 13th and 25th month cohorts.
FR-06: Claim Ratio must be calculated as:

Claim Ratio = Total Claims Paid / Total Premium Collected

FR-07: NPS must be segmented as:

* Promoters (9–10)
* Passives (7–8)
* Detractors (0–6)

FR-08: Dashboard must support export to PDF and Excel.

---

## 6.2 Non-Functional Requirements

* Data refresh frequency: Daily (Scheduled)
* Dashboard load time: < 5 seconds
* Role-based security implementation
* Data model must follow Star Schema
* System availability: 99%

---

# 10. Acceptance Criteria

The project will be considered successful when:

1. All KPIs match validated sample calculations.
2. Drill-down functionality works without data mismatch.
3. Star schema relationships are correctly implemented.
4. Role-based access control is tested and approved.
5. Dashboard performance meets defined SLA.
6. User Acceptance Testing (UAT) sign-off is received.
